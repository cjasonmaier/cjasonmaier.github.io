How to Play

Simple to Learn.
Endlessly Deep to Master.

01

Slide to Match

Drag blocks across the board to match the glowing target shapes. Every swipe costs a move, so plan wisely.

02

Carve & Earn

Complete a shape and it's carved away — and you earn moves back. Chain multiple matches in one slide for a combo bonus.

03

Rotate Freely

Rotate the entire board to find the matching angle. Rotations are always free — use them when you need to.

04

The Bonus Block

Occasionally, a bonus block appears randomly on your board. Include it in a Carve and your move meter fills completely.
